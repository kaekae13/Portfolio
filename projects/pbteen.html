  • TOOLS | Adobe Photoshop

THE CHALLENGE

Redesign the Storage & Organization Landing Page for the Pottery Barn Teen Spring 2022 season, creating corresponding email & social assets as a complete marketing campaign.

DATE

Jan. 2022

SERVICES

Redesign of site interface. Creation of email & social assets to align with the site experience and create a cohesive marketing campaign.

For this project, it was most important to create a highly functional and visually appealing shopping experience for the customer. 

With the aim of creating an elevated shopping experience for the customer, I placed emphasis on clean layout design and intentional curation of images. I worked closely with marketing and ecommmerce teams to ensure that high-performing products were featured and all key categories were captured.
